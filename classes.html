CLASSES & PRICING

To view our live schedule or to sign up for a class, please visit MindBody or download the Yoga Harmony App.

HOT 60, HOT 75, HOT 90

We offer three types of Hot 26 classes, varying between 60 minutes, 75 minutes, and 90 minutes. These classes are named on based on the number of minutes (Hot 60, Hot 75, and Hot 90). Postures are performed in a heated environment to maximize the physical benefits both internally and externally. The series is great for all levels.

HARMONY & ALLEGRO

The Harmony is a vinyasa class synchronizing breath with movement. It will offer a balance of strength, flexibility, awareness, and endurance. The Allegro is a more dynamic interpretation of our Harmony class, with a more vigorous flow. All levels are welcome to these classes! While still warm, these classes are not as hot as the traditional Hot Series.

MIX-UP

The Mix-Up is a 75-minute vinyasa vibe and an ever-changing playlist with requests welcome. All levels are welcome to this classes! While still warm, the Mix-Up is not as hot as the traditional Hot Series.

TENDER LOVIN' HARMONY

The Tender Lovin’ Harmony is a restorative class, working through gentle postures with the use of props and modifications. There will also be a deeper focus on breath leading to deeper relaxation. This class is a bit gentler on the body, and is a great option for those more sensitive to heat and dealing with acute or chronic issues. Reservations are required for Tender Lovin’ Harmony classes.

COMMUNITY CLASS

Our weekly Community Class serves the Nashville community, with contributions going to a selected charity in place of our regular class rates. Package holders may attend. A minimum $5 donation is required.

YOGA THERAPY

Yoga therapy uses yoga to treat illnesses of all kinds: physical, emotional, and mental. Prvate sessions with our trained Yoga Therapist are available by request.
