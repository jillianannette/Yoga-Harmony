TEACHER TRAINING

Yoga Harmony & Mindful Motion Yoga School are proud to offer two exciting teacher training programs! Our programs build a solid foundation in the principles, alignment, and philosophy of Yoga. Training is held one weekend a month, and takes place over a series of months. We believe it takes more than a few weeks to assimilate the vast knowledge you will gain surrounding all eight limbs of the Yogic Path. We aim to serve as mentors to our students long after the program ends.


While enrolled in the program, unlimited yoga is available at Yoga Harmony and Mindful Motion. Trainees will earn their Yoga Alliance Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT) credentials.
