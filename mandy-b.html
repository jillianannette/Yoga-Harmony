MANDY BRODERICK

"I look forward to sharing my knowledge with others in helping reach their fullest growth potential in their personal practice."

I first experimented with yoga back in college, over 10 years ago. At first, a bit timid, realizing I needed to broaden my fitness to include some 'stretching' for injury prevention. A competitive runner and dancer in high school, athletics and remaining active was always part of my daily life. It wasn't until becoming a mom for the first time in 2007, that I revisited yoga and took it seriously. It was way more than stretching! A prenatal Iyengar class had me hooked! Since then I have followed my dream to become certified, become a devoted student (and journaler!), and love all things yoga! You will also see me teaching Spin and Sculpt classes, but, as I tell all my students, make sure and do your yoga with all your other training, it's so important! I enjoy incorporating various types of yoga styles in my classes, including the 26 poses, Vinyasa and Iyengar. I thank my teachers for their warmth and knowledge as I continue to grow as a student and teacher.

I look forward to sharing my knowledge with others in helping reach their fullest growth potential in their personal practice. I look forward to learning from you. May peace and joy be with you,

Shanti,
Mandy Broderick
