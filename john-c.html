JOHN CORE

"I realized just as a musician tunes an instrument, yoga tunes the self — both physical and mental."

I am a lifelong musician and yoga teacher, born and bred in southwest Louisiana. I have always been an adventurer, and my Yoga quest began in 2011 after a skiing accident left my shoulder nearly completely immobile. What began as a physical healing journey quickly transcended into a mind and bodly transformation.

I realized just as a musician tunes an instrument, yoga tunes the self — both physical and mental. The practice of yoga has provided me with a path of strength, surrender, and peace, and through my teaching, I hope to share these gifts with the world around me. I believe I can empower people with yoga and I can bring mindfulness and light to all who attend my classes.

Namaste,
