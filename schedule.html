TEACHER TRAINING

Yoga Harmony & Mindful Motion Yoga School are proud to offer two exciting teacher training programs! Our programs build a solid foundation in the principles, alignment, and philosophy of Yoga.

CERTIFIED 200HR RYT

March 2016 - September 2016

Certification includes:

  • Hot 26
  • Vinyasa
  • Restorative

200HR and 300HR students train side by side for the first seven months of the program. Students will gain a deeper understanding of the 26 Hot Yoga postures, Vinyasa poses and how to arrange them for maximum benefits, and restorative poses for greater relaxation. Yoga history, anatomy, and sutras are just a few of the other subjects covered during the 200HR program.

Cost: $3,000
Payment plans are available.

CERTIFIED 300HR RYT

March 2016 - December 2016

Certification includes:

  • Hot 26
  • Vinyasa
  • Restorative

Continuing the education gained in the seven months of the 200HR program, our 300HR students experience an introduction to Yoga Therapy for those who may be interested in pursuing that credential with International Association of Yoga Therapists. Yoga Alliance does not credential Yoga Therapists. In respect to the Yoga Alliance standards, we teach a deeper understanding of anatomy and yoga so as to help you tailor your teaching to the needs of an individual client.

Cost: $4,500
Payment plans are available.
